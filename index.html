01 Warm-Up Review複習暖身 0:00 – 0:10 ▼
⚡ Quick Flash
Flip through CVC short vowel word cards from last session at a fast pace. Child calls out the vowel sound and reads the word. Keep it brief — this is activation only, not new teaching.
快速翻閱上堂短母音字卡，孩子讀出母音及單字。目的是暖身啟動記憶，不作新教學。
02 New Content — Blends & Silent Letters新內容 — 字尾拼讀與默音字母 0:10 – 0:40 ▼
🃏 Introduce & Sort
Introduce each pattern one at a time using the new word cards. For -mp and -nt: say the base word, then add the ending — child repeats. For gh and kn: show the card, explain the silent letter rule simply ("the gh is sleeping — we don't say it"), child reads the whole word. After all four patterns are introduced, mix the cards and do a quick sort — child groups by ending or pattern.
逐一介紹四個拼讀規律。-mp 和 -nt：先唸詞根再加字尾，孩子跟讀。gh 和 kn：展示字卡，簡單說明默音規則（「gh 在睡覺，不發音」），孩子讀整個單字。四種規律介紹完畢後，混合字卡讓孩子依規律分類。
-mp：jump · lamp · camp · bump · dump · hump · damp · stomp
-nt：tent · dent · hint · rent · mint · hunt · ant · punt
gh silent：ghost · night · light · right · fight · eight
kn silent：knee · knit · knock · knot · knife · know
03 Brain Break活動休息 0:40 – 0:45 ▼
👏 Clap the Pattern
Teacher calls out a word. Child claps once for -mp, twice for -nt, covers ears for silent gh/kn. 10 words, fast pace. Pure physical transition — no cards needed.
老師唸單字，孩子拍一下表示 -mp，拍兩下表示 -nt，捂耳朵表示 gh/kn 默音。共十個字，節奏快，純粹作為過渡活動，不需字卡。
04 Sentence Fishing Game句子釣魚遊戲 0:45 – 1:10 ▼
🎣 Sentence Fishing
Lay all 14 sentence cards face-down in the "pond" (tray or blue paper). Child fishes with the magnet rod. Teacher reads the caught sentence aloud — child tracks with finger and repeats the target word. New pattern cards (01–10) score 2 stars. Review cards (11–14) score 1 star. Collect as many as possible in the time.
將14張句子卡面朝下放在「魚塘」中（托盤或藍色紙）。孩子用磁鐵釣竿釣句子，老師讀出句子，孩子用手指追蹤並重複目標單字。新內容字卡（01–10）得2顆星，複習字卡（11–14）得1顆星，限時收集。
05 Build It, Read It拼字讀句合併遊戲 1:10 – 1:20 ▼
🔀 Mixed Game
Shuffle blend word cards face-down. Child flips one, reads it (with help if needed), then finds the matching sentence fishing card from the caught pile and points along as teacher re-reads it. One star token per correct round. Fast, keeps both skills active together.
混合字尾字卡面朝下洗牌。孩子翻開一張讀出單字，再從已釣到的句子卡中找到對應句子，手指追蹤老師重讀。每輪答對得一顆星。節奏快，同時鞏固兩項技能。
06 Wind-Down — Hide & Seek結尾活動 — 藏字卡找找看 1:20 – 1:30 ▼
🔍 Hide & Seek Word Cards
Child picks 4–5 favourite word cards from today and hides them around the room while teacher covers eyes. Teacher searches, finds each card, reads it aloud with exaggerated surprise. Child confirms if correct. Star sticker reward at the end.
孩子選出今天最喜歡的4–5張字卡，趁老師遮眼時藏在房間各處。老師四處尋找，找到後誇張地大聲讀出字卡，孩子確認是否正確。最後貼上星星貼紙作獎勵。
